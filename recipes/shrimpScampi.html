Shrimp Scampi

Description

Shrimp scampi is a delicious dish that is easy to make for a quick dinner

Ingredients

  • 8 Ounces Linguine
  • 2 Tablespoons unslated butter
  • 1 Pound peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • freshly grated parmesan to garnish

Steps

  1. Boil water to cook the pasta
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet
  3. Add shrimp, garlic, red pepper flakes. Cook 2-3 minutes
  4. stir in wine and lemon juice;remove from heat and stir in pasta
  5. serve, garnished with parmesian
